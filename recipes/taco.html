Taco

Desciption

Taco, the ultimate Friday night treat! This recipe is quick, easy, and always a hit.

  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can corn
  • 200g shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Lettuce
  • Taco shells or Tortillas
  1. Brown the ground beef in a skillet. Add the taco seasoning and water according to package directions. Keep warm.
  2. Place the corn and cheese in separate bowls. Chop the cucumber, tomatoes, and onion and place them in separate bowls.
  3. Place the sour cream, salsa, and guacamole in separate bowls. Arrange lettuce leaves on a plate.
  4. Heat the taco shells according to package directions.
  5. Let everyone assemble their own tacos and enjoy!
